The Cansbridge Fellowship

Each year, we accept a cohort of highly ambitious Canadian students to experience a summer internship in Asia, attend a week-long conference in the Silicon Valley, and join a network that lasts a lifetime. We believe the mark of a true leader is their ability to build, manage, and innovate across borders, for and with a global community.

PART01

Silicon Valley Experience

The Fellowship Kicks off with a one-week experience in the Bay Area. Fellows stay in a "hacker house" in San Francisco and spend the week builidng close relationships with their cohort.

PART02

4 Month Asia Internship

Fellows fly out to their self-organized internships in Asia. Fellows go abroad to push them out of their comfort zones, $10,000 accelerate thier personal growth, and show them possibilities.
